West Kootenays Climate Considerations

The West Kootenays' unique climate—with mild, wet winters and dry summers—creates specific challenges for tree care. This variable climate leads to vigorous tree growth but also increases risks of disease, storm damage, and drought stress. Our approach to tree care is tailored to these local conditions, ensuring your trees thrive in our specific environment.
