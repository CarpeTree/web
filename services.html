Soil & Root Health

We analyze and improve your soil conditions to optimize root health, ensuring your trees have a strong foundation for growth and longevity.

Pruning

Our professional pruning services enhance tree structure, health, and aesthetics while promoting safety and longevity through careful technique.

Tree Planting

We ensure successful tree establishment through proper species selection, planting techniques, and initial care tailored to your local environment.

Risk Assessments

Our comprehensive evaluations identify potential hazards to protect your property and loved ones from tree-related risks.

Wildfire Risk Management

We assess and mitigate wildfire risks around your property through strategic tree management, defensible space creation, and fuel load reduction to protect your home and family.

Storm & Fire Damage

Quick response and professional handling of storm or fire-damaged trees to prevent further property damage and restore safety. Our before and after results show how we can transform damaged trees into safe, aesthetically pleasing assets.

Tree Removal

When necessary, we safely and efficiently remove trees with minimal impact to surrounding landscape and structures.

Milling

Transform removed trees into valuable lumber, giving them new life as sustainable wood products for your home or projects. Our portable sawmill creates high-quality dimensional lumber from trees right on your property.

Wildlife Snags

Convert hazardous trees into valuable wildlife habitat, supporting local biodiversity while maintaining safety. These modified trees provide essential nesting cavities and foraging sites for woodpeckers, owls, and other cavity-nesting species.

Cat & Drone Rescue

Professional and humane retrieval of cats, drones, and anything of value stuck in difficult locations. Whether it's a pet up a tree, a drone caught in branches, or valuables down a crevasse, our climbing technicians can safely recover it.

Canopy Science

Research and monitoring services in tree canopies to support conservation, education, and scientific understanding.

Treehouse & Walkways

Custom design and installation of tree-supported structures that blend with nature while preserving tree health.
