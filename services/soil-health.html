Soil & Root Health Services

Strengthening trees from the ground up.

A tree's foundation is its soil. In urban settings, soil conditions can be compromised by compaction and disturbances. Our approach goes beyond traditional methods, emphasizing biodiversity and sustainable support for root systems.

Soil Health Solutions

  • Soil Analysis: Comprehensive testing of soil composition and health
  • Decompaction: Relief of soil compaction to improve oxygen flow and root expansion
  • pH Balance: Adjustment of soil acidity for optimal nutrient uptake
  • Organic Matter: Integration of high-quality organic materials
  • Moisture Management: Solutions for drainage and water retention

Root System Care

  • Root Flare Excavation: Restoring critical oxygen flow around the base of the tree
  • Root Mapping: Non-invasive assessment of root distribution
  • Root Pruning: Selective pruning for construction or pathway conflicts
  • Growth Direction: Guidance for proper root development

Biological Enhancement

  • Mycorrhizal Applications: Introduction of beneficial fungi for enhanced nutrient uptake
  • Biochar Integration: Long-term soil structure improvement
  • Compost Tea: Liquid biological amendments for soil life
  • Mulching: Proper application of organic mulch for soil health

Urban Site Solutions

  • Management of limited soil volume
  • Solutions for hardscape conflicts
  • Structural soil installations
  • Root barrier implementation
  • Subsurface aeration systems

Monitoring & Maintenance

  • Regular soil health assessments
  • Seasonal care recommendations
  • Irrigation system evaluation
  • Long-term monitoring programs
