Treehouse & Recreational Installations

Tree-friendly structures that connect people with nature.

Overview

Our treehouse and recreational installation services combine arboricultural expertise with creative design to create safe, beautiful, and tree-friendly structures. Whether you're dreaming of a backyard treehouse for children, a serene adult retreat, or a simple tree swing, our team ensures that installations are both structurally sound and respectful of tree health.

We specialize in attachment methods that minimize damage to trees, allowing them to continue growing naturally while safely supporting your installation. Our designs accommodate tree movement and growth, ensuring both the longevity of your structure and the health of your trees.

Our Tree-Friendly Approach

Traditional construction methods can damage trees through improper attachments, compression, and girdling. Our specialized techniques use dynamic fastening systems, proper load distribution, and careful planning to protect tree health. We assess each tree's species, age, health, and structure to determine the most appropriate installation method that will minimize impact while ensuring safety and stability.

Our Installation Approach

  • Tree-Friendly Attachments: Non-invasive mounting systems that protect tree health
  • Dynamic Design: Structures that accommodate tree growth and movement
  • Custom Fabrication: Tailored hardware solutions for specific projects
  • Safety Standards: Construction that meets load-bearing requirements
  • Sustainable Materials: Guidance on appropriate, long-lasting material choices

Each installation begins with a thorough assessment of your trees and site, followed by a collaborative design process that balances your vision with the practical considerations of tree health and structural safety. We handle all aspects of the installation, from custom hardware fabrication to final construction.
