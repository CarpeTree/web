Storm Damage Mitigation

Emergency response and proactive preparation for extreme weather events.

When storms strike, trees can become hazards to people and property. Our experienced emergency response team provides rapid, professional service to address damaged trees and mitigate risks. We also offer proactive care strategies to reduce storm damage vulnerability before extreme weather strikes.

Emergency Response Services

  • 24/7 Storm Response: Rapid deployment for hazardous tree situations
  • Risk Assessment: Quick evaluation of immediate dangers and priorities
  • Emergency Stabilization: Temporary supports for compromised trees
  • Hazard Mitigation: Safe removal of dangerous limbs and debris
  • Property Protection: Strategic operations to prevent further damage

Post-Storm Recovery

  • Damage Documentation: Comprehensive assessment for insurance purposes
  • Restoration Pruning: Specialized techniques to rehabilitate damaged trees
  • Structured Removal: Safe elimination of unsalvageable specimens
  • Cleaning & Clear-Up: Thorough site restoration services
  • Replanting Guidance: Expert advice on storm-resistant species selection

Preventive Storm Preparation

  • Vulnerability Assessment: Identification of at-risk trees and branches
  • Crown Reduction: Strategic pruning to decrease wind resistance
  • Structural Pruning: Improving tree architecture for better storm resilience
  • Deadwood Removal: Elimination of potential projectiles before storms
  • Support System Installation: Cabling and bracing for vulnerable trees

⚠️ If you're experiencing a tree emergency:

  1. Ensure personal safety first—stay away from damaged trees
  2. Call emergency services (911) if power lines are involved
  3. Contact us immediately at 778-655-3741 for rapid response
  4. Document damage with photos when safe to do so
  5. Avoid DIY tree work on storm-damaged trees
