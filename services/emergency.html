Emergency Tree Care

Rapid response when nature strikes.

When storms or unexpected events cause tree damage, our emergency services ensure swift, safe action to mitigate risks and preserve as much of your tree as possible. Our team is ready to respond to urgent situations 24/7.

Immediate Response Services

  • Hazard Assessment: Quick evaluation of immediate risks
  • Emergency Stabilization: Temporary support for compromised structures
  • Debris Clearing: Safe removal of fallen branches and debris
  • Property Protection: Measures to prevent further damage
  • Safety Perimeter: Establishment of safe zones around hazards

Post-Emergency Care

  • Damage Assessment: Detailed evaluation of tree health and structure
  • Recovery Planning: Development of long-term care strategy
  • Structural Repairs: Professional correction of storm damage
  • Preventive Care: Recommendations to reduce future risks

When to Call Emergency Services

  • Storm-damaged trees threatening structures
  • Broken or hanging branches
  • Uprooted or leaning trees
  • Trees interfering with power lines
  • Sudden tree failure or collapse

Our Emergency Response Protocol

  • 24/7 availability for urgent situations
  • Rapid deployment of qualified arborists
  • Priority scheduling for hazardous conditions
  • Coordination with emergency services when needed
  • Documentation for insurance purposes

⚠️ If you're experiencing a tree emergency:

  1. Ensure personal safety first
  2. Call emergency services if power lines are involved
  3. Contact us immediately at 778-655-3741
  4. Keep a safe distance from damaged trees
