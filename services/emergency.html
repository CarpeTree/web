Storm & Fire Damage Mitigation

Emergency response and proactive preparation for extreme weather events and fire disturbances.

When storms strike or fires damage your property, trees can become hazards to people and property. Our experienced emergency response team provides rapid, professional service to address damaged trees and mitigate risks. We also offer proactive care strategies to reduce vulnerability before extreme weather or fire events.

Emergency Response Services

  • 24/7 Storm & Fire Response: Rapid deployment for hazardous tree situations
  • Risk Assessment: Quick evaluation of immediate dangers and priorities
  • Emergency Stabilization: Temporary supports for compromised trees
  • Hazard Mitigation: Safe removal of dangerous limbs and debris
  • Property Protection: Strategic operations to prevent further damage

Post-Disaster Recovery

  • Damage Documentation: Comprehensive assessment for insurance purposes
  • Restoration Pruning: Specialized techniques to rehabilitate damaged trees
  • Structured Removal: Safe elimination of unsalvageable specimens
  • Cleaning & Clear-Up: Thorough site restoration services
  • Replanting Guidance: Expert advice on disaster-resistant species selection

Preventive Preparation

  • Vulnerability Assessment: Identification of at-risk trees and branches
  • Crown Reduction: Strategic pruning to decrease wind resistance
  • Structural Pruning: Improving tree architecture for better resilience
  • Deadwood Removal: Elimination of potential fire fuel before disaster events
  • Support System Installation: Cabling and bracing for vulnerable trees
  • Defensible Space Creation: Strategies to protect properties from wildfire
