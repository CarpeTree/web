Specialty Tree Services

Unique canopy access services that go beyond traditional tree care.

Our team's advanced climbing and rigging skills enable us to offer specialized services that few others can provide. From animal rescue to scientific research support and custom installations, we bring professional arboricultural expertise to unique canopy challenges.

Cat, Drone & Raptor Rescue

  • Animal Rescue Training: Specialized techniques for safely handling stressed animals
  • Careful Approach: Minimizing additional stress through gentle handling methods
  • Appropriate Equipment: Specialized gear for different rescue scenarios
  • Rapid Response: Priority scheduling for urgent animal situations
  • Wildlife Coordination: Connections with licensed wildlife rehabilitators when needed

Canopy Science & Research Support

  • Scientific Collaboration: Supporting university research and citizen science projects
  • Equipment Installation: Placement of monitoring devices, cameras, and sensors
  • Sample Collection: Access to specific canopy locations for specimen gathering
  • Experimental Setup: Installation of research infrastructure in trees
  • Biodiversity Surveys: Assistance with canopy flora and fauna inventories

Treehouse, Swing & Walkway Installation

  • Tree-Friendly Attachments: Non-invasive mounting systems that protect tree health
  • Dynamic Design: Structures that accommodate tree growth and movement
  • Custom Fabrication: Tailored hardware solutions for specific projects
  • Safety Standards: Construction that meets load-bearing requirements
  • Sustainable Materials: Guidance on appropriate, long-lasting material choices
