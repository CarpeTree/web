From Tree to Treasure – Custom Milling Services

Transform fallen or removed trees into premium, custom-milled wood products.

Give your trees a second life through our custom milling services. We take value-added wood from your property and transform it into anything from bespoke furniture to unique home accents—while honoring the natural grain and character of each tree.

Custom Processing Options

  • Precision Milling: Expert processing of both hardwoods and softwoods
  • Custom Dimensions: Cuts tailored to your specific project needs
  • Design Consultation: Professional guidance on wood selection and usage
  • Species Expertise: Knowledge of local wood characteristics and applications
  • Quality Control: Careful attention to grain patterns and wood stability

Sustainable Value

  • Resource Recovery: Converting potential waste into valuable materials
  • Local Sourcing: Utilizing wood from your own property
  • Carbon Storage: Keeping carbon locked in wood products
  • Waste Reduction: Maximizing the use of available timber
  • Heritage Preservation: Maintaining the story and legacy of your trees

Product Possibilities

  • Furniture Components: Table tops, benches, shelving
  • Architectural Elements: Beams, mantels, trim
  • Decorative Items: Live edge slabs, cutting boards
  • Outdoor Features: Garden elements, pathway materials
  • Custom Projects: Specialized items to your specifications

Our Process

  • Initial wood assessment and consultation
  • Development of milling strategy
  • Careful processing and cutting
  • Proper drying and storage recommendations
  • Finishing suggestions and resources

Additional Services

  • Wood Storage: Proper stacking and drying facilities
  • Transportation: Safe movement of materials
  • Artisan Connections: Network of local craftspeople
  • Project Planning: Support for custom wood projects
