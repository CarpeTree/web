Value-Added Wood Services

Transform your trees into enduring beauty through expert milling, craftsmanship and upcycling.

When a tree must be removed, our value-added wood services honor its legacy by transforming it into useful, beautiful materials. Custom Milling & Lumber

  • On-Site Processing: Portable bandsaw mill brings lumber production to your property
  • Custom Dimensions: Slabs and dimensional lumber cut to your specifications
  • Species Expertise: Knowledge of ideal applications for each wood type
  • Quality Control: Proper stacking, stickering, and end-sealing for optimal results
  • Project Consultation: Guidance on wood characteristics and appropriate uses

Stump Grinding

  • Complete Removal: Elimination of visible stump to below ground level
  • Selective Grinding: Options to maintain portions for landscaping features
  • Root Management: Treatment of larger surface roots as needed
  • Surface Restoration: Debris removal and area preparation for new uses
  • Equipment Selection: Right-sized machinery to minimize site impact

Artistic & Creative Options

  • Chain Saw Artistry: Transforming stumps or logs into decorative features
  • Slab Preparation: Creating unique table tops and artistic pieces
  • Specialty Cuts: Book-matched sets, burl sections, and feature elements
  • Unique Edge Treatments: Preserving live edges and natural features
  • Artisan Connections: Referrals to local craftspeople for custom projects

Environmental Benefits

  • Carbon Sequestration: Keeping carbon locked in wood products for generations
  • Waste Reduction: Converting potential waste into valuable materials
  • Local Sourcing: Minimizing transportation impacts through on-site processing
  • Forest Conservation: Reducing demand for commercial timber
  • Educational Value: Creating awareness of sustainable wood utilization
