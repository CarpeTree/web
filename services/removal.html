Tree Removal & Wildlife Trees

Thoughtful removals with ecological responsibility in mind.

Tree removal is always a last resort. Whether due to structural instability, disease, or invasive species, we ensure removals are executed safely, efficiently, and with minimal environmental impact. When possible, we transform removal scenarios into habitat opportunities through our wildlife tree program.

Our Removal Process

  • Comprehensive Assessment: Thorough evaluation of all alternatives before recommending removal
  • Safety Planning: Detailed site analysis and risk management strategy
  • Precision Removal: Safe, controlled takedowns using expert rigging techniques
  • Site Protection: Measures to protect surrounding landscape and structures
  • Complete Cleanup: Thorough site cleanup and debris removal

Wildlife Tree Program

When appropriate, we can transform a removal scenario into a habitat opportunity, preserving ecological benefits while ensuring safety.

  • Assessment of wildlife habitat potential
  • Safe reduction of tree height and spread
  • Creation of habitat features
  • Ongoing monitoring and maintenance
  • Educational resources for property owners

When Removal May Be Necessary

  • Severe structural defects that cannot be corrected
  • Advanced disease or decay
  • Storm damage beyond recovery
  • Invasive species management
  • Construction or development requirements

Post-Removal Services

  • Replanting Guidance: Expert advice on replacing removed trees with site-specific selections
  • Stump Removal Options: Various methods to handle remaining stumps
  • Soil Restoration: Preparation for future plantings
  • Habitat Enhancement: Recommendations for supporting local wildlife
